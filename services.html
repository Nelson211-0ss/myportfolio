Core offerings

Service lines

Community & school trainings

Reusable pad and liquid soap production; menstrual hygiene facilitation; safeguarding-aware sessions for SMCs and peer educators.

Access & last-mile pathways

Design inputs for kiosk-style distribution, stocking logic, privacy-respecting outreach, and light monitoring without exposing beneficiaries.

Advocacy & male-ally facilitation

Baraza dialogues, media talking points, and stigma-reduction formats that enlist men without turning girls into spectacle.

Documents & programme design support

TOR review, menstrual budget annexes for WASH tenders, Theory of Change language, and linkage to cited public references where useful.

Speaking & partnership convenings

Panels and keynotes on period poverty entrepreneurship, sanitation rights framing, and school retention mechanics — moderated for dignity-first tone.

How engagements run

Typical projects start with a short needs scan (who delivers sessions, safeguarding baseline, sanitary infrastructure realities), then scoped training or advisory milestones with clear handover artefacts — facilitator notes, BOM lists, safeguarding lines, suggested follow-up touchpoints.

Longer alliances may layer peer certification, district-level menstrual budget templates, or joint donor reporting drafts. Use the contact page with sector, geography, budget band, and calendar windows.
